About K.P. Hostel Modasa

Our Mission

The K.P. Boys Hostel in Modasa is dedicated to providing a supportive and academically conducive environment for the youth of the Kadava Patel Samaj. Our mission is to foster personal, academic, and social development of our residents, ensuring they thrive in their educational journeys.

Our Vision

To create an institution where students not only receive excellent educational resources and accommodation but also develop lifelong skills and values, nurturing them into successful individuals contributing to society.

Our Values

  • Academic Excellence
  • Personal Growth
  • Community Support
  • Integrity and Responsibility
